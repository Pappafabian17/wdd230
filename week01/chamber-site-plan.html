The Plate Chamber Mar del Plata

This name reflects the city of Mar del Plata and emphasizes its importance as a cultural and economic hub.

Site Purpose

1. Community Building: The website aims to create a vibrant community of small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in Mar del Plata, facilitating networking and collaboration among businesses.

2. Job Creation: The platform serves as a resource for connecting job seekers with local businesses, fostering employment opportunities and contributing to the city's economic growth.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month to promote networking among local businesses?
  • Where can I find information about membership and benefits offered by the chamber?
  • What resources are available for entrepreneurs looking to start a business in Mar del Plata?
  • How can I get involved in community initiatives that support local businesses?

Color Schema

1. Paynes Gray: #545e75 - Used for headings and main navigation elements.

2. Argentinian Blue: #63adf2 - Used for backgrounds and buttons to create a fresh, inviting look.

3. Uranian Blue: #a7cced - Used for accent elements, such as borders and highlights.

4. Yinmn Blue: #304d6d - Used for footer backgrounds and secondary navigation.

5. Air Superiority Blue: #82a0bc - Used for text and call-to-action buttons to draw attention.

Typography

Headings: Big Shoulders Inline (for a modern and friendly look)

Body Text: Roboto (for clear and easy readability)
